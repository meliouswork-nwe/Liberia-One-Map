About Liberia OneMap

What is OneMap?

The Liberia OneMap is a Demonstration Operational Prototype (DOP) for a unified national web portal integrating open geodetic control data, LiDAR/DEM terrain, satellite imagery, and planimetric GIS layers under a single authoritative interface.

Purpose & Audience

Designed for donor presentations, government stakeholder demonstrations, and pre-investment showcasing. Serves as a proof-of-concept for the national Liberia Geospatial Data Infrastructure (LGDI).

Bomi County Pilot Data

27,897 buildings · 2,449 road segments · 201 waterline features · 37 water bodies · 1,195 land-use polygons · County boundary · 4 geodetic control monuments.

Institutional Anchors

Liberia Land Authority (LLA) · Department of Lands, Surveys & Cartography (DLSC) · LISGIS · Lake Piso Solutions · University of Liberia GITA Center.
