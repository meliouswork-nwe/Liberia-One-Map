Participate in Liberia OneMap

Contribute data, partner with the initiative, or request access to the national geospatial portal.

How to Participate

📤 Submit Data

Share geospatial datasets, survey control points, or aerial imagery with the OneMap data catalog. All submissions reviewed against ISO 19115 metadata standards before publication.

🏢 Institutional Partnership

Government ministries, NGOs, universities, and private sector organizations are invited to formally partner with the LGDI initiative through the Liberia Land Authority.

💻 Technical Contribution

GIS developers, web engineers, and geospatial professionals can contribute to the open-source portal codebase hosted on GitHub.

📡 CORS Network Expansion

Support the expansion of the LGR CORS network to all 15 counties — contact the LLA to sponsor monument establishment in uncovered counties.
