Standards & Interoperability

ISO 19115 Metadata

All datasets catalogued with ISO 19115 geographic metadata standard — enabling discovery by international spatial data clearinghouses including AfriGEOSS and GSDI.

OGC Web Services

WMS (map visualization), WFS (feature download), WCS (raster coverage), and CSW (catalog search) endpoints for every published dataset.

EPSG / CRS Registry

Formal EPSG registration of the LGR projected coordinate system recommended to ensure interoperability with QGIS, ArcGIS, and global GIS platforms.

Open Data Licensing

All LGDI datasets published under Creative Commons Attribution (CC-BY 4.0) or equivalent open license — enabling free use by NGOs, researchers, and private sector.
