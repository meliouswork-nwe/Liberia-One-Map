About Liberia OneMap

A Demonstration Operational Prototype (DOP) for the Liberia Geospatial Data Infrastructure — the first unified national geospatial portal in Liberia's history.

Background

The LGDI Vision

Liberia has historically lacked a publicly accessible national geospatial data infrastructure. The Liberia OneMap portal addresses this gap by aggregating geodetic control data, topographic mapping, satellite imagery, and administrative boundaries into a single authoritative web-accessible platform — modeled on successful OneMap initiatives in Southeast Asia and Africa.

Report Reference

This portal is documented under reference LGREF-ONEMAP-2026-001. Source data is drawn from the LIDP portal (liberiaidp.net/monuments) and documented in report LGREF-LIDP-2026-001 by Matt Elious, ASPRS Emeritus Member.

Data Custodian

Lake Piso Solutions (LPS), a Liberian technology and geospatial company, operates the Liberia Infrastructure Data Portal (liberiaidp.net) and serves as data custodian for the LIDP monument dataset. LPS's CEO is Isaac Vah Tukpah.
